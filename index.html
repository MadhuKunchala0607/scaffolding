    What Is Covered Under The Scaffolding Insurance?
    • Under public liability insurance, $5 - $10 million, and $20 million coverage is provided; higher limits are available on client request.
    • Coverage for sub-contractors is also available on client request.
    • Both commercial and residential scaffolding is covered under scaffolding insurance.
    • High-level works over 10 meters.
    • High-risk situations, including mine sites, marine, and similar scenarios, are also covered.
    What Is Covered Under The Scaffolding Insurance?
    • Worker to worker claims for contractors and sub-contractors who injure themselves on your job site
    • Accidental drop of equipment from height
    • Collapse of scaffolding due to poor construction
    • Collapse of scaffolding due to unforeseen weather events
    • Claims of people on the site for trips and falls
    • Damage of built structures that scaffolders are working on
    • Businesses/companies getting sued by local state workers compensation due to claim made by contractors and sub-contractors
    • Fines laid for occupation health hazards and safety breaches
